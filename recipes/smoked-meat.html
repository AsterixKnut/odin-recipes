Smoked Meat

Description

Have you ever been exploring a lush and beautiful planet, only to be interrupted by the roaring growl of your empty stomach? Well fret not, for here is a simple but tasy recepie for smoked meat. The ingredients can be found on almost any planet in the galaxy, so go find them and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 meat of any kind
  • 1 carbon or condensed carbon

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in a Nutrient Processor
  2. Power it on and wait for the meat to be smoked
  3. Enjoy!
