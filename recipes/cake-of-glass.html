Cake of Glass

Description

Don't let the name fool you. This cake is not made of literal glass, despite it's name. It is however one of the most difficult cakes a baker can aspire to cook. Most go their whole life without ever being able to create a single cake of glass. This makes cakes of glass extremely valuable, and any cook who get a chance to bake one should attempt so. Interestingly, all these cakes have been baked by Travellers, the most known being made by the legendary chef and food critic Cronus.

Ingredients

  • 1 wailing batter
  • 1 anomalous jam
  • 1 cream or bone cream

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in a Nutrient Processor
  2. Power it on and watch the ingredients melt together like silicate powder in a Refiner
  3. Enjoy!
