Creamed Organ Soup

Description

It's not often your taste buds crave the creamy texture of creamed organ soup, and even if they don't, this recipe is still a must for any culinarian to learn. It is a higly valued dinner for those few who enjoy it (mostly Vy'keen gluttons), so there are tonnes of units to be earned if you are not put off by intestines and the likes. The recipie is quite simple, so long as you have all the required ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 stewed organs
  • 1 creamy sauce

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in a Nutrient Processor
  2. Power it on and watch the stew and creamy sauce mix
  3. Enjoy!
